Maanasa Sivashankar (Mahn-sa)

Specialist in Service Design & Strategy

Shaping how organizations effectively delivery services to people

I am a designer, researcher and urbanist currently leading the service design team at the Mayor's office of Innovation, where I set the direction for city services to be more effective and efficient; shapes design and research strategy for the Mayor's highest priority issues. Before MOI, I worked in New York City as a consultant on Fortune 100 projects with organizations like Johnson & Johnson, J.P.Morgan private Bank, Penske Trucking Company, Meta, Google and as in-house consultant for The World Bank.
